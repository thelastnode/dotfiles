* Ido
We want smarter minibuffer completion, so let's enable ido and make it
available everywhere.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(require 'ido)
(ido-mode 1)
(ido-everywhere 1)
#+end_src

** Flex matching
Let's turn on fuzzy matching for ido to find stuff faster.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq ido-enable-flex-matching t)
#+end_src
